Welcome to Tennis Basics

Learn the fundamentals of tennis and start your journey in this amazing sport!

Why Learn Tennis?

  • Improve your physical fitness and coordination.
  • Boost your mental focus and strategic thinking.
  • Enjoy a fun and social activity with friends and family.

Basic Rules of Tennis

Here are the fundamental rules to get started:

  • The game starts with a serve. The server must hit the ball diagonally across the net into the opponent's service box.
  • Points are scored when the opponent fails to return the ball within the court boundaries.
  • A game is won by scoring at least four points with a two-point advantage.
  • Matches can be best of 3 or 5 sets, depending on the competition.

Essential Equipment

Before you hit the court, make sure you have the following:

Racquet

Choose a racquet that suits your skill level and playing style.

Ball

Use high-quality tennis balls for better bounce and durability.

Shoes

Wear tennis-specific shoes to enhance movement and avoid injuries.

Clothing

Comfortable, breathable clothing is ideal for playing tennis.
