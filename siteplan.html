Tennis Enthusiast Club Website Plan

Site Name

Tennis Enthusiast Club

I chose this name because it's clear and perfectly reflects what I want to convey: a place for all tennis lovers. I want people interested in the sport to find a space to connect and share their passion for tennis.

Site Purpose

This site will be an information hub for tennis enthusiasts. It will have sections on the basics of tennis, tips to improve skills, places to play in the area, and details about events and tournaments. Additionally, there will be a membership page where users can sign up, access exclusive content like training videos, and participate in a forum to discuss tennis.

Scenarios

  • What are the best exercises to improve my backhand in tennis?
  • Where can I find a tennis court near me?

Color Schema

I’ve chosen the following colors for the site:

Main color: #2E8B57 - I’ll use this for headings, buttons, and key elements.

Accent color: #4682B4 - I’ll use this for subheadings, links, and hover effects.

Typography

The fonts I will use on the site are:

  • Font for headings: Arial, sans-serif (for H1 and H2 titles)
  • Font for body text: Georgia, serif (for paragraphs and descriptions)
